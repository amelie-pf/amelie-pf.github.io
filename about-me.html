Hi there, I´m Amelie!

Currently, I am studying Mobile Media at the Stuttgart Media University. The focus of my studies is mainly on UI / UX, because I would like to work in this field after I gratuated. I really enjoy being creative, especially when it comes to solving problems with good and intuitive design and creating simple things.


Additionally, in my studies I have learned the basics in software development, web development and app development. Understand the technical side and background of a good design is essential for me.


I spend a lot of time in nature with friends, especially at lakes or by the water or travelling because I am very curious about new cultures and places, which I find insanely inspiring and I develop a lot of creative ideas.

My values

harmony

For me, harmony is much more than a feeling, it is often the small things that make harmony complete. Harmony describes a balance of different parts that fit together, complement each other and together form a balanced unit.

curiosity

Children are curious and want to understand how something works. I have never lost this aspect in the course of my life. The excitement of trying new things and the desire to learn new insights drives me constantly. For me, it's like an "engine" for the development of my projects.

honesty

From my point of view, honesty stands for sincerity, openness, straightforwardness and fairness. Not only in private, but also in the professional environment, this is essential. Honesty requires a high degree of self-reflection and sovereignty, as it also includes admitting one's own mistakes and dealing with them openly.

quality

Quality means for me that work will be completed with with the highest requirement to the fullest satisfaction. This includes not only the completion of actual work but also reliability and transparency.
