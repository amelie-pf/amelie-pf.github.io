Hi there, I´m Amelie!

I am a Mobile Media student at the Stuttgart Media University. I'm all about User Interface and User Experience - you know, the stuff that makes digital experiences seamless and satisfying. I thoroughly enjoy employing my design skills to tackle problems, even though it can be challenging at times. Finding creative solutions is incredibly rewarding, and I always strive to make the design process as smooth and efficient as possible. My goal is to build a career in this field after I graduate and hopefully make the digital world a little prettier and easier to navigate.

In addition to my design skills, I've also got a solid foundation in software development, web development, and app development. I may not be a code wizard, but I know enough to bring some tech magic to my designs. Because let's be honest, without the tech, the design is just a pretty picture.

When I'm not glued to my computer screen, I like to unplug and get some fresh air. Nature is my happy place, and I'm a big fan of hanging out by lakes or waterfronts. And speaking of hanging out, I love exploring new cultures and places. There's nothing quite like the thrill of discovering something new and inspiring.

I've also been playing the piano since the third grade. Well, I may not be able to read sheet music (my teacher always thought I needed glasses, but here I am, still sheet-less and clueless how to read them), I've found that playing the piano helps me tap into my creativity in other ways and taught me valuable skills that can be applied to design. Like the importance of precision and attention to detail. Every note and every finger placement matters when playing a piece, just like every pixel and every element matters in design. It is like playing the piano requires finding the perfect balance between melody, harmony, and rhythm, just like designing requires finding the perfect balance between form and function - right?

Feel free to check out my portfolio and see what kind of creative projects I've been up to. If you like what you see, let's chat about how we can create something magical together.

My values

harmony

For me, harmony is much more than a feeling, it is often the small things that make harmony complete. Harmony describes a balance of different parts that fit together, complement each other and together form a balanced unit.

curiosity

Children are curious and want to understand how something works. I have never lost this aspect in the course of my life. The excitement of trying new things and the desire to learn new insights drives me constantly. For me, it's like an "engine" for the development of my projects.

honesty

From my point of view, honesty stands for sincerity, openness, straightforwardness and fairness. Not only in private, but also in the professional environment, this is essential. Honesty requires a high degree of self-reflection and sovereignty, as it also includes admitting one's own mistakes and dealing with them openly.

quality

Quality means for me that work will be completed with with the highest requirement to the fullest satisfaction. This includes not only the completion of actual work but also reliability and transparency.
